Wuthering Waves Rotation & Endstate Matrix Planner

A lightweight, serverless, and fully client-side theorycrafting tool for Wuthering Waves.

This tool allows players to visualize complex character rotation sequences, calculate projected Endstate Matrix scores using a dynamic waterfall algorithm, and share plans and rotations with other players instantly without downloading or uploading any files.

🛠️ Project Status
Version: 0.1 (Open Beta)


✨ Features

Endstate Matrix Dashboard: Dynamically calculate your projected and actual boss scores. The built-in "waterfall" algorithm automatically spills overkill damage into subsequent bosses to show exactly how far your teams will progress.

Drag-and-Drop Rotation Canvas: Plan your combos visually. Spawn action nodes, organize them in a free-form brainstorming canvas, and lock them into a linear timeline.

Zero-File Sharing: Click "Share" to encode your entire rotation and timeline data into a secure, compressed URL string. Share your strategies instantly via Discord or Reddit without risking malicious .json file downloads.

100% Serverless: Everything runs locally in your browser. No databases, no backend tracking, and instant load times.
